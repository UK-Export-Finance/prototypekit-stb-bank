Bank payments portal – Version 2

Bulk reporting payments with spreadsheet upload

User guide

  1. Follow the instructional content in the prototype, the goal is to upload a spreadsheet with the payments made to UKEF from the bank and submit it to UKEF.

    (Hint: you are not expected to enter any data, that will be provided pre-filled on the 'Tab 2' sheet of the spreadsheet).
  2. The first time of uploading the spreadsheet will always result in seeing the error page. Any other upload after that will result in an error-free upload.
  3. When looking at the error page you can refer to the 'Tab 2' sheet of the spreadsheet.
  4. View any of the first 4 issued facilities (sorted by UKEF facility ID).
  5. Use the Clear data link in the footer before starting the journey again.
